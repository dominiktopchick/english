Police

What is the Police?

The police are a group of people who work together to keep our community safe. They help us when we are in trouble and make sure people follow the laws. Police officers are our friends, and they are always there to help. What does the Police do? They respond to calls when someone needs help. They investigate crimes and try to find those who committed them. They help keep order on the roads and ensure traffic safety. They also help in emergencies, such as fires or natural disasters. They help people who are lost or those who need assistance.

Types of Police

Patrol Police: They patrol the streets and respond to calls.

Detectives: They investigate complex crimes.

Traffic Police: They monitor road safety.

Special Forces: They respond to emergencies.

How to Contact the Police In different countries?

In Ukraine, the emergency number for the police is 102.

In the USA people usually call 911.

In the United Kingdom people call 999.

In Canada people call the same number as in the USA, 911.

In France people use a short number, 17.

In Germany people call 110.

Why is police important?

Police is important because it helps regulate people, stop fights, and help people that are in trouble.
